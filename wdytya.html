Who Do You Think You Are?

During my time as art editor of the magazine, as a result of audience research we moved away from a focus on the celebrity stories of the TV programme to a brand which had a nostalgic and relatable feel.
As well as the magazine design, my role included: design of all marketing assets, branded merchandise, cover mounts and exhibition graphics and brochure for the ‘Who Do You Think You Are? Live’ trade show at Olympia.
